# Interview question bank

One act per message. Pick the 3-4 strongest questions per act for this user;
adapt wording to what you already know. React to answers before moving on.
"Skip" is always a valid answer. Record distilled facts to `profile.md` and
targets to `goals.md` after every act.

## Act 1 — Snapshot (who am I working with?)

1. What do you do for money today, and how many hours a week does it really take?
2. Outside that, how many focused hours a week can you genuinely give to your goals — the honest number, not the aspirational one?
3. What are you unusually good at (top 2-3)? What do people ask you for help with?
4. What tools/stacks are you fluent in vs. learning? (This calibrates trainings later.)
5. What's your energy pattern — when in the day are you sharpest?

## Act 2 — Money

1. Roughly what did you make in the last 12 months (a range is fine)?
2. Number you'd be *proud* to make in the next 12 months, and the number you'd *settle* for?
3. Rank your realistic income streams for this year (salary, freelance, products, content, other) by how much you believe in each — top 3 only.
4. What's the single biggest thing standing between you and the proud number?
5. Any hard money constraints (debt payments, runway, must-not-drop-below)?

## Act 3 — Projects

1. List everything currently in flight — even the half-dead ones. One line each.
2. Of those, which ONE would change your year the most if it shipped and worked?
3. Which should be killed or frozen so the winner gets its hours? (Killing is a win — say so.)
4. Anything new you've been itching to start? Should it beat something on the current list, or wait?
5. For the top project: what does "shipped" look like, dated?

## Act 4 — Skills & trainings

1. What 2-3 skills, if you leveled them up, would most raise your income or ship speed this year?
2. For each: rate yourself 1-10 today, and what would an 8 let you do that you can't now?
3. How do you actually finish learning things — building alongside, quizzes/challenges, short daily reps, deep weekend dives?
4. What's a skill you tried to learn before and bounced off? What killed it? (Design trainings to dodge exactly that.)

## Act 5 — Metrics & cadence

1. Which 3-5 numbers will tell us the year is working? (e.g. monthly income, users, subs, shipped features, weekly deep-work hours.) For each: current value and 12-month target.
2. How often do you want check-ins — weekly, biweekly? Which day?
3. What does falling off the wagon usually look like for you, and what's the earliest visible symptom?
4. When you hit a quarterly target, how do you want to celebrate? When you miss, what should I do — push harder, re-plan, or ask why?

## Close

- Read back the top 3 goals with metric + target + deadline. Ask for ONE correction.
- Save everything. Offer: "Want the 12-month plan now?"
